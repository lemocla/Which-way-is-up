Delivery and returns policy

When you ordering prints and original artwork from Which Way Is Up, the following delivery and returns policy will apply:

1. Delivery policy

All delivery are free across the the United Kingdom (UK). If you are ordering or wish to be delivered to an address outside the United Kingdom (UK), please email us at claireci.lemonnier@gmail.com or by using the contact form.

All orders are processed within 2 working days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days and we will contact you by email or phone should there be a significant delay in the shipment and delivery of your items.

2. Damages and items lost in transit

If any prints or original items have been damaged in transit, please contact us by email at claireci.lemonnier@gmail.com or by using the contact form within 2 working days of the receipt of your items, .

3. RETURNS (refunds and exchanges)

If you are unhappy with your item, please contact us within 30 days of your purchase by email at claireci.lemonnier@gmail.com or by using the contact form to arrange for a refund or an exchange. Postage for returning the items will be at your cost.

Original artwork cannot be exchanged and are only eligible for a refund.

To be eligible for a refund, prints and artwork must be returned in a re-saleable condition. That means your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. To complete your refund or exchange, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.

4. Refunds (if applicable)

Once your returned item have been received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If your refund has been approved, it will be processed and credit applied to the credit card or original method of payment within 7 working days.

If you have paid for the delivery of your items, the cost of delivery will be refunded, but not the postage incurred to return the items.

5. Sale items

We do not offer refunds for sale items, unless they are faulty.

6. Exchange (if applicable)

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item for the same item, please send us an email at claireci.lemonnier@gmail.com and use our contact form.
